About Job Sansar

Job Sansar is a modern AI-powered job portal designed to connect job seekers (candidates) with job providers efficiently and intelligently. Our platform leverages advanced AI techniques to match resumes with job descriptions, helping both employers and candidates save time and find the best fit.

Key Features

  • AI Matching: Uses BERT-based sentence embeddings to rank CVs against job descriptions.
  • Role-Based Login: Separate dashboards for Candidates and Job Givers for a personalized experience.
  • Secure Uploads: Candidates can upload CVs, and Job Givers can upload job postings.
  • Domain-Based Matching: Jobs and CVs are matched according to their domain (Engineering, IT, Healthcare, Education, etc.).
  • Feedback System: Logged-in users can provide feedback directly to the admin panel for continuous improvement.
  • User Management: Admin can view all registered users and submitted feedback in one place.

How It Works

1. Job Givers upload job descriptions specifying the required domain.
2. Candidates upload their CVs with the corresponding domain.
3. Our AI engine analyzes job descriptions and CVs, computes similarity scores, and lists the best matches.
4. Both parties can quickly access the best-fit matches for faster hiring and job application processes.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between job seekers and employers using AI-driven solutions, making the recruitment process faster, smarter, and more accurate.
